Yourong

I remember the first time the four of us (one quit shortly afterwards) gathered in the conference room on the seventh floor discussing how we should start this project – they all chose the back-end modules, leaving me with the interface. That’s how my UX career kicked off. In retrospect, I gained most of my understandings in UI and UX theories through this project, by investigating myriads of websites and apps, and reading books like Don’t Make Me Think and Non-Designer’s Design Book.
